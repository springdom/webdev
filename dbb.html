Cruise Dunedin by Bike $59

Discover the sights of Dunedin with this ALL DOWNHILL bike ride into the city.
  • Fitness level required - Low
  • Distance - 15km (downhill and flat)
  • Approx. time to complete - 1hr with no stops

We meet you outside the Port Chalmers Public Library (a 5 min stroll from your ship) at 8:30am with your bikes, then drive you up to the highest sealed road in the area at 330mASL.

We give you a map with a suggested route into Dunedin.

From here it's an all downhill ride to the centre of Dunedin with plenty to see along the way.

Take in the stunning views of the Otago Peninsula across Dunedin harbour. Stop at Baldwin Street, the world's steepest.

Visit the Dunedin Botanical Gardens, the Otago University with its heritage buildings, or the Dunedin Railway Station and St Clair Esplanade and Beach.

Ride as little or as much as you want, you have the use of the bike all day. When you're finished, lock it up at a prearranged time and place.
