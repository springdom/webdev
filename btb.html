Bike the Bays $35

Check out the tranquility of the Otago Harbour with this flat and easy ride to the beach at Aramoana.
  • Fitness required - low to moderate
  • Distance - 16km return
  • Approximate time to complete - 2 hours

We meet you outside the Port Chalmers Public Library (a 5 min stroll from your ship) at 8.30am and give you your bikes and a map.

Start your ride in Port Chalmers wharf and bike along a quiet, sealed road towards Aramoana Beach at the entrance of the Otago harbour(8km).

This beautiful beach is home to some of Dunedin's famous wildlife such as the Hooker sea-lion, NZ Fur seals, Albatross and Yellow Eyed Penguins.

Go wildlife spotting, relax on the beach or walk out to the end of the breakwater. Whatever you do the scenery here is stunning.

Return via the same road back to your ship and leave your bikes locked at a prearranged time and place.
